Jay Kristoff

Jay Kristoff grew up in Canberra, Australia and moved to Melbourne the first chance he got. He has a degree in the arts and has over two million books in print and has been published in over thirty-five countries. He has co-authored two series with Amie Kaufman, and solo authored four series.
