Birthstone Books


I found this wonderful article idea on Instagram where you find books with the cover colours or titles matching your birthstone. I was born in February, so my birthstone is Amethyst!


I scoured the internet for books with purple covers or titles that relate to Amethyst, and I was surprised that there weren't as many options as I had hoped. However, I found a few interesting choices that I can't wait to read and share with others who have the same birthstone. This was a fun and creative way to discover new books that I might not have encountered otherwise. What is your birthstone? Maybe it's time to go through your shelves and the interent for a birthstone book for your next read.

The Bone Witch
by Rin Chupeco
Image from Penguin Books Australia Tea is a bone witch. The power she has makes her an outsider from her family and community. Everyone fears her. Except an older bone witch who takes her in and trains her to be an asha. SOmething dark encroaches on her new life and Tea must make a choice to save her new life, or destroy it.

A Curse of Blood and Bloom
by Santana Saunders
Image from Goodreads Evanthe Sideris, is a trained Stone Holder of Meraki, likely to earn a place in the next Noonsnight, but that isn't enough for her. Aero Vouvali, has learned above all else to never trust a woman. She owes him an island. He owes her his sword. Together, they get so much more than they bargained for.

Goldeline
by Jimmy Cajoleas
Image from Jimmy Cajoleas In the woods of the Hinterlands, where magic is as real as stories are, Goldeline travels from camp to camp with Gruff and his bandits, getting by on the things they steal. But someone is after Goldeline. Now Goldeline must summon all the courage and magic to escape her pursuers.
