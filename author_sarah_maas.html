Sarah J. Maas

Born in New York in 1986 and raised on the Upper West Side, Sarah attended Hamilton College, where she studied creative writing. She graduated magna cum laude and met her husband, Josh, during her time there. After college, the couple moved to California. Sarah's first and most popular work, Throne of Glass, is a "what if" story that explores the question: What if Cinderella were an assassin?
