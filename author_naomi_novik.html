Naomi Novik

Naomi Novik lives in New York with her family. She studied English literature at Brown University and did graduate work in computer science at Columbia University. Naomi is a founder of the Organization for Transformative Works and the Archive of Our Own. She has won the British Fantasy, Locus, Mythopoeic and Nebula Awards, and received nominations for the Hugo and World Fantasy Awards.

Source: Goodreads and Naomi Novik
