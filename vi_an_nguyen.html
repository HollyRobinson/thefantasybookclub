Vi-An Nguyen

Vi-An Nguyen is a Brooklyn-based Art Director at Penguin Random House, where she creates playful and engaging graphics for book covers and publishing products. With a passion for the intersection of art, editorial, and storytelling, Vi-An's career began in journalism, writing for publications like Texas Monthly and The Knot. She later transitioned to design, offering freelance services and advancing within Penguin Random House, where she now holds an Art Director role. A graduate of Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism, she also studied graphic design and typography at the School of Visual Arts.

Source: The Knot
