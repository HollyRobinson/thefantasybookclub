Kuri Huang

Kuri Huang, a freelance illustrator from Chengdu, China, currently lives in New York. She has received recognition from the Cannes Lions award ceremony in 2019, Creative Liaisons 2019, 3x3 International Illustration Annual, American Illustration, China Animation, and Comic Competition Gloden Dragon Award. She learned illustration as a student and worked for Onmyoji (a Chinese game company) and Guomai (a Chinese publishing company) before graduation. This is where she says her artistic career began.

Source: Girls Club Asia
