September Pick

The pick for September is House of Earth and Blood by Sarah J. Maas. Bryce enjoyed her life in Cresent City until a murder shook the city to its core and her world was altered forever. Two years later the attacks start again, resulting in angel Hunt Athalar being assigned to watch her every move. Together they will try to unravel the mysteries of the city, which could spell doom for everyone.

Book for August

Agnieszka is at peace in her village. However, lurking nearby is an enchanted forest home to an ageless wizard known only as The Dragon. Her village depends on his protection but it comes at a price. A young woman in the village is sacrificed to the wizard and has to serve him for 10 years. Agnieszka has no idea that she is next.
