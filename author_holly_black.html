Holly Black

Holly Black is a bestselling author of contemporary fantasy, known for her vivid storytelling and acclaimed works like Tithe: A Modern Faerie Tale and The Folk of the Air series. She co-created The Spiderwick Chronicles with Tony DiTerlizzi, a beloved series adapted into a film and a 2024 television show. Holly lives in New England with her family and cats in a house with a secret library.

Source: blackholly.com
