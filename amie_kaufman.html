Amie Kaufman

Amie Kaufman is from Australia but also grew up in Ireland. She has undergraduate degrees, with honours, in law, history and literature. She has a master's in conflict resolution and is getting a PhD in creative writing. She currently lives in Melbourne by the sea. She has co-written 5 series with Jay Kristoff and Meagan Spooner and has written 2 series by herself and written a few short stories for anthologies.
