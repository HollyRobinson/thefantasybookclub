Cliff Nielsen

Cliff Nielsen is a freelance illustrator, graphic novel artist, and motion designer who lives in Los Angeles, California. His illustrations have been recognised for their excellence by the Society of Illustrators, Print, and Spectrum, founded in 1901. Cliff has studied traditional and digital illustration techniques, and his work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibitions worldwide. He graduated valedictorian from the Art Centre College of Design in California. He has been an international speaker on digital art and a judge for the Society of Illustrators during his career.

Source: Shannon Associates
