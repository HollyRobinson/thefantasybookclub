Micaela Alcaino

Micaela Alcaino, born in Sydney, Australia, embodies a rich cultural tapestry with her Chilean father and German/Argentinian mother. Since settling in London in 2013, she has carved a remarkable path in book cover design. Her journey began with positions at Penguin Random House UK and Harper Collins UK. Transitioning to freelance work in 2019, Micaela's artistry reached new heights, earning her recognition as one of the Bookseller's Rising Stars in 2021. Her crowning achievement came in 2022 when she clinched the prestigious Designer of the Year award at the British Book Awards. Even in 2024, Micaela continues to be celebrated, again nominated for the Designer of the Year award.

Source: micaelaalcaino.com
