Elizabeth Lim

Elizabeth Lim grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and was a professional film and video game composer before becoming an author. She graduated from Harvard College and the Julliard School. She now lives in New York City with her husband and two daughters. Elizabeth has authored the Six Crimson Cranes duology, The Blood of Stars duology and 3 of the Disney Twisted Tales books.

Source: Penguin Random House
